About

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Center for the Study of the Metropolis (CEM) — FAPESP/Cepid/USP, supervised by Prof. Ursula Peres (EACH/USP). I hold a Ph.D. in Political Science from the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE, 2025), with an M.A. and B.A. from the same institution and an exchange program in International Relations at the University of Coimbra (2016).

Previously, I was a substitute professor of Political Science at the Federal University of the São Francisco Valley (UNIVASF, 2022–2024) and a collaborating professor in the Professional Master in Sociology (ProfSocio/UNIVASF, 2023–2025). I currently serve as an invited researcher in the project Intergovernmental Transfers and Brazilian Federalism at the Institute for Applied Economic Research (IPEA), Brasília.
